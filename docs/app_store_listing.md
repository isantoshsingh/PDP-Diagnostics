### What Prowl Does

Prowl is an automated product page monitor that catches broken functionality on your Shopify store before it costs you sales. Every day, Prowl scans your product pages like a real customer would — loading the page, checking that everything displays correctly, and testing the actual Add to Cart flow. When something breaks, you get an email alert with a screenshot, a plain-language explanation of the problem, and a suggested fix.

Product page errors are one of the most common — and most invisible — causes of lost sales. A broken variant selector, a missing Add to Cart button, images that won't load, or a price that doesn't display can stop customers from buying without generating any error on your end. Most merchants only discover these issues after noticing a drop in conversions — days or even weeks later. Prowl catches these problems the same day they appear.

### Key Features

- **Daily product page monitoring** — Automated scans check your pages every day for broken functionality
- **AI-powered issue detection** — Smart analysis confirms real problems, filters out false alarms, and generates clear explanations
- **Purchase flow testing** — Prowl clicks Add to Cart and verifies items actually reach the cart, just like a real customer
- **Screenshot evidence** — Every alert includes a screenshot so you can see exactly what's broken
- **Actionable fix suggestions** — Each issue comes with a plain-language explanation and a suggested fix you can act on immediately
- **Works with any theme** — Compatible with all Shopify themes, no code changes or setup required

### What Prowl Detects

Prowl monitors for the product page issues that directly impact your ability to sell:

- Missing or broken Add to Cart buttons
- Add to Cart buttons that don't actually add items to cart
- Broken variant selectors (size, color, style options)
- Missing or broken product images
- Missing product prices
- JavaScript errors that affect page functionality
- Theme rendering errors
- Slow page load times

### How It Works

Add your product pages to Prowl and monitoring starts automatically. Every day, Prowl visits each page in a real browser, checks for structural issues, and runs a full purchase flow test — selecting a variant, clicking Add to Cart, and confirming the item reaches the cart. AI analysis reviews the results, confirms real issues, and generates merchant-friendly explanations.

When a high-confidence issue is found, you receive an email alert immediately — no waiting for a second scan to confirm. Each alert includes a screenshot of the page, a description of what went wrong, and a specific suggested fix. You can acknowledge alerts to stop repeat notifications, and issues are automatically resolved when Prowl confirms they've been fixed.
